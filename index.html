About Me

Jeff McDonald is a seasoned professional raised in the Tulsa Metropolitan Area, with diverse experience gained in Lesotho, South Korea, and Puerto Rico, spanning across technology, education, decentralized networks and non-profit sectors.

Interests

Passionate about blockchain technologies—like NEM, Symbol, Bitcoin, Ethereum, Morpheus, WaterFall—governance, trustless innovations, artificial intelligence (AI), aliens, and financial independence. Alongside these interests in technology, I prioritize health, longevity and life extension, education, spirituality, fitness, self-reliance, meditation, truth, science, and Christian values, blending personal growth with a vision for positive societal change.
