Apostille: A Blockchain Notarization and Timestamping Service

Published on: January 15th, 2017

The paper introduces Apostille, an innovative blockchain notarization service built on the NEM platform. Unlike traditional timestamping systems that offer static, one-time notarizations, Apostille provides dynamic, updatable, and transferable timestamps. Its key innovation lies in creating deterministic private keys for notarized data, allowing for the creation of dynamic accounts that can prove authenticity, show ownership, and record updates. The system leverages NEM's features such as namespaces, mosaics, messages, and multisignature accounts to offer a comprehensive notarization solution. Potential use cases span various industries, including automotive (for car titles and registrations), digital media licensing, luxury goods authentication, and legal contract management. The authors position Apostille as a significant advancement in blockchain technology, offering new features and business opportunities beyond conventional proof-of-existence services.
